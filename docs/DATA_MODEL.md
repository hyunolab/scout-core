# Nuclear Scout Data Model

## Goal

Nuclear Scout does not collect news.

It discovers and stores changes in the global nuclear industry.

---

## Main Entities

### Article

Original news article collected from media.

### Fact

A unique fact extracted from one or more articles.

### Change

The meaning of the fact.

Example:

- Policy

- Technology

- Investment

- Regulation

- Accident

### Technology

Examples:

- SMR

- TRISO

- Molten Salt Reactor

- Fast Reactor

### Country

Country related to the change.

### Organization

Examples:

- IAEA

- DOE

- EDF

- Rosatom

- Westinghouse
